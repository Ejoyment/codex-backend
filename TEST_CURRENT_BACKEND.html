3. What to Check in Render

Go to Render Dashboard: 1. Open: https://dashboard.render.com 2. Click: codex-backend-7utu 3. Select: "Clear build cache & deploy" 5. Click: "Deploy" 6. Wait: 3-5 minutes 7. Come back and test again This will force a fresh deployment and cancel any stuck ones.
